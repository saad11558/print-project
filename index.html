How It Works

From Poll to Pickup in 4 Simple Steps

A seamless flow connecting students, class reps, and the print shop.

1
👨‍🏫

CR Creates a Poll

Class Rep uploads the document, sets the price, adds a payment QR, and launches the print poll.

2
📱

Students Scan & Pay

Students see the poll, scan the QR code, and pay instantly through any UPI app.

3
🖨️

Print Shop Processes

Paid orders automatically enter the queue. The shop prints in bulk — efficient and organized.

4
📦

Pickup When Ready

Get a notification when your prints are ready. Walk in, grab them, and go — no waiting.
